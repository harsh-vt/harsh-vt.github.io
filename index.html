Description

This course will introduce the students to the fundamentals of data analysis - descriptive, univariate and multivariate - required to work with data emerging from transportation engineering/planning domain. The participants will also be exposed to a few real-world surveys, data collection and analysis exercises to make conclusions about some transportation problems.


Course Contents

Introduction to data types; Sample Design; Data Aquisition; Descriptive Analysis; Probability theory; Descriptive statistics; Theory of inefernce; Multivariate Statistics; Regression Modelling.


Software Component: Introduction to R


Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of the course, the participants will be able to appreciate the fundamentals of applied statistics, complexities involved in transportation data analysis, and discuss solutions incorporating data uncertainty.
